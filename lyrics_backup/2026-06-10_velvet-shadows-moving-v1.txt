[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Velvet shadows moving on the wall
I only listen when I hear you call
A sudden spark is burning in the dark
I want to leave a never-ending mark

[Verse 1]
The busy world is fading out of sight
I lead you to the quiet of the night
A single lantern glowing on your face
I pull you closer in a slow embrace

[Pre-Chorus]
Your eyes are pulling secrets out of me
Like rivers running down into the sea
I feel the heavy beating in my chest
This close to you is where I want to rest

[Chorus]
These velvet shadows moving on the floor
Are making me want everything and more
I hold you close to feel the steady heat
And listen as our quiet whispers meet

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, falling deeper in the warm and slow
There is nowhere else I want to go

[Verse 2]
I trace the gentle lines upon your skin
And wonder where to let the night begin
Your fingers running softly through my hair
We breathe the tension in the midnight air

[Bridge]
Velvet shadows dancing on the wall
I want to catch you every time you fall

And let my hands rest softly on your hips

Under the beauty of the endless skies
